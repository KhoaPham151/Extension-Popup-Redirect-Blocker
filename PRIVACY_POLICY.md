# Privacy Policy for Popup & Redirect Blocker

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## Overview

Popup & Redirect Blocker is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect:**
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Cookies or tracking data
- Any data that leaves your device

## Data Storage

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Extension enabled/disabled state
- Blocking statistics (count of blocked items)
- User preferences (notification settings)

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your browser.

## Permissions Explained

- **declarativeNetRequest**: Used to block network requests to known ad/popup domains. No browsing data is collected.
- **storage**: Used to save your preferences locally on your device.
- **notifications**: Used to optionally notify you when popups are blocked (can be disabled).
- **host_permissions (<all_urls>)**: Required to block popups and redirects on all websites. No data is collected or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer.

## Consent

By using this extension, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
